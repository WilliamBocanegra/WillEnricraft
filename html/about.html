About me

William Bocanegra also knows as WillEnricraft is a Youtuber making Minecraft animations.

William was born in the years two thousand in Montreal. During his first school year, he had difficulty keeping up with his classmates because he was and still is dyslexic. At the age of 11, he loved messing around with the Minecraft's game folder with his iPod touch slowly having a love for technology. He started watching videos online and how to's. At the age of 13, his dad bought him a laptop, on his birthday. His school was kinda boring, knowing that, he wanted to learn something new: how to make mods and learn how to build computers. He also made videos with his youtube channel. He self-taught himself how to make animation, learn to use a 3d software called Blender, edit videos, manipulating photos, dismantle his laptop out of curiosity and made a couple of games. At the age of 16, he finally had enough money to build a computer, without any help. His channel blew up when he posted a Minecraft animation: Skywars No Mercy.
